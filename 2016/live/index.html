Location Info


The event will take place in the Ohio Union (1739 North High Street). Please be aware of our lockdown: no one will be permitted to enter or exit the building from 11:30 PM to 7:00 AM on Sunday, November 20th. During the lockdown, attendees should remain in the Grand Ballroom and Great Hall except to use the bathroom (and in case of emergency).

The Grand Ballroom and Great Hall are the designated hacking areas. The Showcase (Sunday, 2:30 PM) will be held in the Performance hall. In addition, there are several other spaces around the Union where you can find tech talks, relaxation retreats, and gaming - refer to the schedule below!
